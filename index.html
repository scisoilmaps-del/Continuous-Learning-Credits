The Producer and 20% of the Producer's staff who actively make management decisions for the Enrolled Acres must participate and provide Evidence of learning credits annually.
Annual Credit Targets
§5.8.1 — Farms with annual gross revenue of $150,000 or more 24 credits/yr
↳ At least 8 of the 24 credits must be on-farm research 8 research
§5.8.2 — Small holdings or beginning farms with revenue under $150,000 16 credits/yr
↳ No on-farm research minimum applies
Credit Allocation (Table 5.8)
Relevant agricultural or regenerative webinar (1 hr)1 credit
Online short-form content, agronomic consultation, or group meeting (1–3 hrs)2 credits
In-person meeting, field day, conference, online content >3 hrs, annual soil testing3 credits
On-farm research & development — test strips, flora/fauna ID, low-impact ag tech4 credits
💡 First-year note (§5.8.3): Enrollment, completing the Farm Plan, executing baseline soil testing, and participating in the soil testing interpretive support meeting satisfy the first-year requirement. Renewal requires evidence of full annual credits.
